Anthentic French Culture

A place to feel good, to relax for a moment before everyday life continues.

Indulge in the authentic flavors of France at our charming French restaurant and café. Our menu has been carefully crafted to bring the essence of Paris to your plate, from the freshest croissants to flavorful tarte flambée.

Come to our authentic café for a short vacation to France. Whether breakfast, lunch or dinner, let yourself be pampered by our Maisoneés. In our La Maisons you will be transported to the heart of Paris with stylish furnishings and an inviting ambience. From the hustle and bustle of our café to the lounge-like atmosphere of our dining room, our aim is to offer you a truly unforgettable experience.
